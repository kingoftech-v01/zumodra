Refund Policy

1. Terms

FreelanHub's refund policy is designed to protect both clients and freelancers in cases where work does not meet agreed specifications or projects are not completed as promised. Refunds are processed on a milestone basis, meaning payments are evaluated individually based on the deliverables and acceptance criteria defined for each milestone.

Clients may request refunds when freelancers fail to deliver work, deliver work that materially differs from specifications, or violate platform policies during project execution. Requests must be submitted through our dispute resolution system with supporting evidence including project requirements, communication records, and delivered work samples.

The escrow system provides protection by holding funds until clients approve completed work. If disputes arise, funds remain in escrow while our review team examines evidence from both parties. Refund decisions are based on objective evaluation of whether deliverables meet the originally agreed terms and professional quality standards.

2. Limitations

Not all situations qualify for refunds. The following limitations apply to refund eligibility to ensure fair treatment of both parties:

  • Work that has been completed according to specifications and approved by the client is not eligible for refunds. Once a milestone is marked complete, payment is released to the freelancer and cannot be reversed.

  • Subjective dissatisfaction with completed work that meets technical requirements does not qualify for refunds. Disputes must demonstrate material failure to meet defined specifications or deliverables.

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the disputed milestone's delivery date. Requests submitted after this period may not be considered unless extraordinary circumstances apply.

Platform service fees are generally non-refundable as they cover transaction processing costs. In cases of proven fraud or platform error, fees may be refunded at FreelanHub's discretion. Change of mind or project cancellation after work has begun does not typically qualify for full refunds.

3. Revisions and errata

This Refund Policy may be updated periodically based on operational experience, user feedback, and dispute resolution patterns. We analyze common dispute scenarios to refine our policies and ensure they balance protection for both clients and freelancers fairly.

Material changes to refund eligibility criteria or processing procedures will be communicated through email notification and platform announcements. The updated policy will apply to new projects initiated after the effective date, while ongoing projects generally follow the policy in effect when they began.

We welcome user feedback on refund policies and dispute resolution procedures. If you believe a refund decision was made in error or have concerns about policy application, you may request a secondary review through our support team. Policy updates reflect our commitment to continuous improvement of the platform experience.

4. Site terms of use modifications

To request a refund, follow these procedures to ensure your case is properly reviewed and resolved efficiently:

  • Initiate a dispute through the platform's dispute resolution system. Provide detailed explanation of the issue, attach relevant documentation including project requirements, communication records, and delivered work samples.

  • Our review team will examine evidence from both parties within 5-7 business days. Additional information may be requested to make an informed decision. Both parties will have opportunity to present their case.

  • Refund decisions will be communicated via email and platform notification. Approved refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days, returning funds to the original payment method used.

Documentation is critical for refund requests. Save all project communications, specifications, revision requests, and delivery confirmations. Clear, well-documented cases are resolved more quickly. For guidance on documentation requirements, please consult our Dispute Resolution guidelines.

5. Risks

While our refund policy aims to provide fair protection, users should understand that refunds are not guaranteed and are determined based on evidence and policy criteria. The dispute resolution process requires cooperation from both parties and may take several days to complete, which can delay project timelines.

FreelanHub's refund decisions are final and binding unless extraordinary circumstances or new evidence warrants reconsideration. We make decisions based on available evidence and platform policies, but cannot account for all nuances of every situation. Users dissatisfied with outcomes may have limited recourse beyond the platform's internal review process.

To minimize refund-related risks, clearly define project requirements, maintain detailed communication records, and use the milestone system to break work into manageable, verifiable deliverables. Proactive communication and documentation significantly reduce the likelihood of disputes and improve outcomes when issues arise. For fraud prevention measures and related information, see our Payment Terms and Security documentation.
