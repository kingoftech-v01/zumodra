Payment Terms

1. Terms

FreelanHub processes all payments through a secure escrow system to protect both freelancers and clients. When a client funds a milestone, payment is held securely until the work is completed and approved. This system ensures freelancers receive compensation for completed work while giving clients confidence that funds are only released when deliverables meet agreed specifications.

All transactions on the platform are processed through our certified payment processors in compliance with PCI-DSS standards. We support major credit cards, debit cards, and various online payment methods. Payments are denominated in USD, EUR, GBP, and other major currencies, with automatic conversion available at current exchange rates.

FreelanHub charges a service fee for facilitating transactions on the platform. The fee structure is transparent and calculated as a percentage of the project value. Fees are clearly displayed before transaction confirmation and are automatically deducted from payments. Both freelancers and clients may incur fees depending on the transaction type.

2. Limitations

Platform fees and payment limitations ensure fair, transparent transactions while covering operational costs. The following fee structure and limitations apply:

  • Service fees range from 5-20% depending on user tier, project value, and account status. New users typically pay higher fees, which decrease with increased platform activity and positive ratings.

  • Withdrawal limits apply based on account verification level. Verified accounts can withdraw up to $50,000 per month, while unverified accounts have lower limits. Processing times vary by payment method.

  • Currency conversion fees may apply when transacting in currencies different from your account's primary currency. Exchange rates are updated regularly and displayed before transaction confirmation.

Minimum and maximum transaction amounts may apply depending on payment method and region. Payment processors may also impose their own fees for certain transaction types, including international transfers and credit card processing.

3. Revisions and errata

FreelanHub reserves the right to modify payment terms, fee structures, and processing policies to reflect operational costs, regulatory requirements, or market conditions. We will provide at least 30 days' advance notice for any fee increases or material changes to payment processing procedures.

Changes to payment terms will be communicated through email notifications and prominent platform announcements. The updated fee structure will be clearly displayed on your account dashboard and during transaction flows. For ongoing projects, fee changes typically apply to new milestones funded after the effective date.

Users who do not accept revised payment terms may complete existing projects under the previous terms but may need to discontinue use of the platform for future transactions. Historical transaction records and fee disclosures remain accessible in your account for reference and tax reporting purposes.

4. Site terms of use modifications

For situations involving payment disputes, cancellations, or quality concerns, please refer to our Refund Policy for detailed procedures. The following points clarify how refunds and chargebacks are handled:

  • Refund requests must follow the dispute resolution process. Funds held in escrow may be refunded if deliverables are not provided or do not meet agreed specifications, subject to review and evidence.

  • Chargebacks initiated through your payment provider may result in account suspension pending investigation. Repeated chargebacks may lead to permanent account termination and reporting to fraud prevention services.

  • Platform service fees are generally non-refundable even when project payments are refunded, as they cover transaction processing and platform infrastructure costs already incurred.

We encourage users to utilize the dispute resolution system rather than initiating chargebacks, as this provides both parties opportunity to present evidence and reach fair outcomes. Chargeback abuse violates our Terms of Use and may result in legal action.

5. Risks

While FreelanHub implements robust security measures to protect financial transactions, users should be aware of payment-related risks. Our escrow system significantly reduces payment fraud risk, but users must still exercise caution. Never share payment credentials, and always verify project details before funding milestones.

Fraud prevention measures include transaction monitoring, identity verification, and behavioral analysis. Suspicious activity may result in payment delays or account holds while we investigate. We may request additional documentation to verify account ownership or transaction legitimacy. These measures protect all users but may occasionally cause inconvenience.

Users are responsible for understanding tax obligations related to platform earnings. FreelanHub may be required to report transaction information to tax authorities in certain jurisdictions. We provide transaction history and reporting tools, but users should consult tax professionals for guidance on their specific situations. For detailed information about payment security, see our Privacy Policy and Security documentation.
