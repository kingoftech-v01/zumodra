Acceptable Use Policy

1. Terms

This Acceptable Use Policy governs your use of FreelanHub and defines the standards of conduct expected from all users, including freelancers and clients. By accessing our platform, you agree to use our services lawfully, professionally, and in accordance with these guidelines.

FreelanHub is designed to facilitate legitimate freelance work opportunities and professional business relationships. You may use our platform to post jobs, submit proposals, communicate with other users, manage projects, process payments through our escrow system, and build your professional reputation through reviews and ratings.

You are responsible for all activity that occurs under your account. You must maintain the security of your login credentials, ensure all information you provide is accurate and current, and comply with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.

2. Limitations

The following activities are strictly prohibited on FreelanHub. Engaging in any of these behaviors may result in immediate account suspension or termination without refund:

  • Posting illegal, fraudulent, or misleading job listings or proposals; engaging in spam, phishing, or unsolicited commercial messages; or attempting to manipulate our platform's ranking, reviews, or search algorithms through artificial means.

  • Harassment, discrimination, threats, or abusive behavior toward other users; posting offensive, defamatory, or inappropriate content; or circumventing our escrow system to process payments off-platform.

  • Unauthorized access to other user accounts, scraping or harvesting data from our platform, uploading malware or viruses, or any activity that disrupts or interferes with FreelanHub's operation or security.

Additionally, you must not impersonate others, create multiple accounts to evade restrictions, share login credentials, or use our platform for any purpose that violates local, national, or international law. We reserve the right to investigate and take appropriate action against violators.

3. Revisions and errata

Violations of this Acceptable Use Policy will be addressed through a progressive enforcement system designed to maintain platform integrity while providing opportunities for users to correct their behavior. The severity of enforcement depends on the nature and frequency of violations.

First-time minor violations may result in a warning and requirement to correct the behavior. Repeated or more serious violations can lead to temporary account suspension, restriction of specific features such as messaging or bidding, or removal of offending content. Severe violations including fraud, harassment, or illegal activity will result in immediate permanent account termination and may be reported to law enforcement.

We reserve the right to investigate suspected violations, review user communications and transactions, and take appropriate action at our discretion. Users subject to enforcement actions may appeal through our support channels, except in cases of severe violations where termination is final.

4. Site terms of use modifications

If you witness or experience violations of this Acceptable Use Policy, we encourage you to report them through our built-in reporting mechanisms. FreelanHub provides multiple ways to report concerning behavior:

  • Use the "Report" button available on job postings, user profiles, messages, and project pages to flag inappropriate content or behavior directly to our moderation team for review.

  • Contact our Trust and Safety team through the support center with detailed information about the violation, including relevant screenshots, messages, or transaction details.

  • For urgent matters involving threats, fraud, or illegal activity, use our emergency reporting hotline available 24/7 to speak with a dedicated safety specialist.

All reports are treated confidentially and reviewed promptly by our moderation team. We do not tolerate retaliation against users who report violations in good faith. Making false reports maliciously is itself a violation of this policy and subject to enforcement.

5. Risks

FreelanHub reserves the right to update this Acceptable Use Policy at any time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or community standards. We will notify users of material changes through email and prominent platform notifications at least 30 days before they take effect.

Your continued use of FreelanHub after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. If you do not agree with the changes, you should discontinue using our services. We encourage all users to review this policy periodically to stay informed about acceptable conduct standards and their responsibilities on our platform.

We are committed to maintaining a safe, professional, and trustworthy marketplace for all users. By working together to uphold these standards, we create an environment where freelancers and clients can collaborate successfully and build lasting professional relationships.
