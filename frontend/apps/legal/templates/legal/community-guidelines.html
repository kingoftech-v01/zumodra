Community Guidelines

1. Terms

FreelanHub is built on the foundation of trust, professionalism, and mutual respect. Our Community Guidelines establish the standards of behavior that create a positive environment where freelancers and clients can connect, collaborate, and succeed together.

Our community values include integrity and honesty in all interactions, respect for diverse perspectives and backgrounds, professionalism in communications and work delivery, accountability for commitments and deadlines, and collaboration to achieve successful project outcomes. These values guide how we expect all users to conduct themselves on our platform.

By joining FreelanHub, you become part of a global professional community. Your actions contribute to the overall reputation and trustworthiness of our marketplace. We expect every user to uphold these guidelines and help maintain the high standards that make FreelanHub a trusted platform for freelance work.

2. Limitations

Professional conduct is essential for successful relationships on FreelanHub. We expect all users to maintain high standards of behavior in their interactions:

  • Communicate clearly, promptly, and respectfully with all users. Respond to messages within reasonable timeframes, provide constructive feedback, maintain professional language, and set clear expectations about availability, deliverables, and timelines.

  • Honor commitments and agreements. Deliver work as promised, meet agreed deadlines, respect payment terms, provide quality work that meets specifications, and communicate proactively if issues arise that may affect delivery.

  • Be honest and transparent in all representations. Accurately describe your skills and experience, provide truthful information in profiles and proposals, disclose conflicts of interest, and represent work samples authentically without plagiarism or misrepresentation.

Professionalism extends to how you handle disputes and disagreements. Approach conflicts constructively, focus on solutions rather than blame, use our dispute resolution process when needed, and maintain respectful communication even when relationships become challenging.

3. Revisions and errata

Certain behaviors are incompatible with our community values and are strictly prohibited on FreelanHub. These include harassment, discrimination, or hate speech based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or other protected characteristics. We have zero tolerance for such behavior.

Additionally prohibited are fraud or deceptive practices including fake profiles, misrepresentation of credentials, bid manipulation, review fraud, or any attempts to game our systems. Also forbidden are abusive communications, threats, intimidation, doxxing, sharing private information without consent, spam, unsolicited commercial messages, or excessive self-promotion outside designated areas.

Users must not engage in platform manipulation, create multiple accounts to evade restrictions, artificially inflate ratings or rankings, or circumvent our fee structure. Copyright infringement, sharing illegal content, and any activity that violates local or international law are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action.

4. Site terms of use modifications

Violations of our Community Guidelines are taken seriously and will result in enforcement actions proportional to the severity and frequency of the violation:

  • Warnings and educational outreach for first-time minor violations. We may provide guidance on expected behavior and require acknowledgment of guidelines before account access is fully restored.

  • Temporary suspensions ranging from 7 to 30 days for repeated violations or more serious infractions. During suspension, access to bidding, messaging, and other platform features may be restricted.

  • Permanent account termination for severe violations including fraud, harassment, illegal activity, or persistent disregard for guidelines. Terminated users forfeit access to all platform features and may lose pending payments or earnings.

All enforcement decisions are made after thorough review by our Trust and Safety team. Users may appeal decisions through our support channels, except in cases of severe violations where termination is final and non-negotiable.

5. Risks

We provide multiple tools to help you report violations and protect yourself from misconduct. Every user profile, job posting, message, and project page includes a "Report" button that allows you to flag concerning behavior directly to our moderation team for immediate review.

When reporting, provide as much detail as possible including specific examples, screenshots, message logs, or other evidence. Our team investigates all reports confidentially and takes appropriate action based on the severity of the violation. We protect reporters from retaliation and take additional action against users who target those who report violations in good faith.

Building a positive community is a shared responsibility. By following these guidelines, reporting violations when you see them, treating others with respect, and contributing constructively to our marketplace, you help create an environment where everyone can thrive. Thank you for being part of the FreelanHub community and upholding the standards that make our platform a trusted place for professional collaboration.
