{% comment %} Getting Started Questions {% endcomment %}

{% trans "Getting Started" %}

{% trans "How do I create an account on Zumodra?" %}

{% trans "Creating an account is simple. Click 'Sign Up' on the homepage, choose whether you're a job seeker, freelancer, or employer, and complete the registration form. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. After verification, you can complete your profile and start using the platform." %}

{% trans "What is KYC verification and why is it required?" %}

{% trans "KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is our identity verification process that ensures all users on the platform are who they claim to be. This bidirectional verification applies to both job seekers/freelancers AND employers, creating a trusted environment for everyone. It involves ID verification, selfie matching, and liveness detection." %}

{% trans "How do I post a job or find a freelancer?" %}

{% trans "To post a job, navigate to your dashboard and click 'Post a Job'. Fill in the job details, requirements, and budget. Your posting will be visible to verified candidates. To find freelancers, use our search filters to browse profiles by skills, experience, location, and ratings. You can then invite freelancers to apply or message them directly." %}

{% trans "Is Zumodra free to use?" %}

{% trans "Zumodra offers multiple pricing tiers to suit different needs. Job seekers and freelancers can create profiles and apply to jobs for free. Employers can choose from our Starter (€15/user), Pro (€25/user), Business (€35/user), or Enterprise plans. Each tier offers different features and capabilities. Visit our Pricing page for details." %}

{% comment %} Verification Questions {% endcomment %}

{% trans "Verification & Trust" %}

{% trans "What documents are required for verification?" %}

{% trans "For Level 1 (KYC) verification, you'll need a valid government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID) and a selfie for liveness detection. For Level 2 (Career) verification, we verify your employment history through automated emails to previous employers and educational credentials through school verification." %}

{% trans "How long does verification take?" %}

{% trans "Level 1 KYC verification is typically completed within minutes using our automated system. Level 2 Career verification may take 2-5 business days depending on response times from employers and educational institutions. You'll receive email updates on your verification status throughout the process." %}

{% trans "What are Trust Badges and how do I earn them?" %}

{% trans "Trust Badges are visual indicators of your verification status. You can earn 'ID Verified' by completing KYC, 'Career Verified' by completing employment/education verification, and 'High Trust' by maintaining excellent ratings and completing multiple successful projects. These badges increase your visibility and credibility on the platform." %}

{% trans "Is my personal information secure?" %}

{% trans "Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption for sensitive data, progressive data revelation (meaning employers only see information relevant to each hiring stage), and we comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations. Your ID documents are verified and then securely stored with access controls. We never share your sensitive information without your consent." %}

{% comment %} Payment Questions {% endcomment %}

{% trans "Payments & Escrow" %}

{% trans "How does the escrow payment system work?" %}

{% trans "Our escrow system protects both parties. When a client hires a freelancer, funds are deposited into escrow via Stripe Connect. The freelancer completes the work and submits deliverables. Once the client approves the work, funds are released to the freelancer. If there's a dispute, our resolution team reviews the case and decides based on evidence from both parties." %}

{% trans "What payment methods are accepted?" %}

{% trans "We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and SEPA direct debit for European customers. Freelancers can receive payouts to their bank account or Stripe account. Enterprise customers can arrange custom payment terms and invoicing." %}

{% trans "What is the refund policy?" %}

{% trans "If work hasn't started, clients can cancel and receive a full refund. Once work begins, refunds are handled through our dispute resolution process. For subscription plans, you can cancel anytime and continue using the service until the end of your billing period. We don't offer partial refunds for subscription cancellations." %}

{% trans "When do freelancers receive their payments?" %}

{% trans "Payments are released from escrow once the client approves the deliverables. After release, funds typically arrive in your connected bank account within 2-5 business days depending on your bank. You can track all your earnings and pending payments in your dashboard." %}

{% comment %} Platform Features Questions {% endcomment %}

{% trans "Platform Features" %}

{% trans "What is Progressive Data Revelation?" %}

{% trans "Progressive Data Revelation is our privacy feature that controls what information is visible at each stage of the hiring process. Initially, employers see basic profile info. After expressing interest, they see contact details. Post-interview, they access references. Only after an offer is accepted do they receive sensitive documents like tax IDs. This protects candidate privacy while still enabling effective hiring." %}

{% trans "How does the AI-powered matching work?" %}

{% trans "Our hybrid ranking engine combines rule-based matching with AI-powered semantic analysis. It considers skills, experience, verification status, trust scores, and job requirements to calculate a match score. Employers can configure the weights given to different factors. The system learns from successful hires to improve recommendations over time." %}

{% trans "Can I use Zumodra for internal hiring?" %}

{% trans "Yes! Business and Enterprise plans include our Multi-Circuit Talent Management feature. This allows you to manage internal mobility (current employees), external recruitment, your talent pool/alumni, and freelancers/contractors all in one platform. Each circuit can have different workflows and approval processes." %}

{% trans "Do you offer e-signature capabilities?" %}

{% trans "Yes, Business and Enterprise plans include DocuSign integration for legally binding e-signatures. You can send offer letters, contracts, NDAs, and other documents for digital signature directly through the platform. All signed documents are securely stored and accessible from your dashboard." %}
